Carrabba's Italian Grill: Chicken Bryan

Why is it called Chicken Bryan?

It's named after the town of Bryan, Texas, where the Carrabba family settled when they arrived from Sicily in the 1890s.They mined their family's Sicilian history, their grandma's recipes, and then invented their own, like Chicken Bryan, originally named Chicken Bryan Texas around 1989–90.
